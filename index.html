Travel must have

When traveling, you need not only the passion but also some skills to help you through your journey.

Sociability

It allows you to make new friends and connections, learn from locals and other travelers, and gain new perspectives on the culture and destination you are visiting. Being sociable also can help you to overcome language barriers and make it easier to navigate unfamiliar places.

Open-minded

It allows you to fully immerse yourself in the culture and experiences of the destination you are visiting. It allows you to be more receptive to new and different ways of thinking and living, and to gain a deeper understanding and appreciation of the people and their way of life.

Autonomy

It allows you to travel at your own pace, to make your own plans and to have a more personalized travel experience. Being autonomous also allows you to be more flexible and adaptable to unexpected situations, such as changes in plans or delays. It also allows you to have more control over your budget and to make better financial decisions.

Adventurous

It allows you to step out of your comfort zone and try new things, whether it's trying a new local dish, participating in an outdoor activity, or visiting an off-the-beaten-path destination. Being adventurous can also allow you to discover hidden gems and unique experiences that you may not have found otherwise.
